Capstone Project :
Whimsical Wizardry

Whimsical Wizardry was my Capstone project at Algonquin College. I worked with a group of 13 people for 8 months to complete a game from start to finish. In that time we made a party game called Whimsical Wizardry. Whimsical Wizardry is a 2-8 player party game, in the game you play as wizards in competion to see who the best among them. I was responsible for programming the frame work and structure of the different game modes.
Whimsical Wizardry is set to release July 1, 2024! Check out the game on steam!

Our first Demo

    After lots of planning and preparing we were ready to make our first demo for Whimsical Wizardry. For our first demo we had a goal to 'find the fun' as early as possible. To achive this we focused on our core concepts and polished them as much as we could. We were pleased to see that our efforts paid off and that we were on the right track to a great game.

Level Up Presentation

    After 8 months of development we were finally ready to show off our game at the 2024 Level Up Showcase in Toronto. We had plenty of people coming up to our booth to try out our game, and everybody that played it had nothing but positive things to say about it. Overall, Whimsical Wizardry was great success at Level Up.
